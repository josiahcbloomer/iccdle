How to play

Guess the ICC-related word or acronym in the given number of guesses.
Words can be between 4 and 7 letters long.
You will be given one guess for each letter in the word, plus one.

Grey = The letter is not in the word
Yellow = The letter is in the word, but in the wrong position
Green = letter is in the correct position in the word
